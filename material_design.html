Additional Material Details

20mm Diameter Aluminum Pipes

These pipes will fit with 3D-printed ABS material, secured using glue. In high-volume production, they would likely be manufactured with ABS for durability and compatibility.

3D Printing Filament

Most machine parts are made from this material. It must mate with aluminum pipes, electrical components, and itself. In high-volume production, injection molding would replace 3D printing for cost-effectiveness and precision.

Arduino Uno

This microcontroller drives the circuitry. It operates servo arms with precise delays. In high-volume production, it would be integrated into a single unit for efficiency.

1-inch Diameter Ball

The ball serves as the final output. While it can be 3D-printed, a steel ball is preferred for added weight and inertia. High-volume production would use a cold heading process.

Miniature Wagon

The wagon activates with input and navigates switchbacks. It is mold fabricated and injection molded for high-volume production.

Breadboard Wires

Wires are chosen based on preference and are made using automated processes for cost-efficiency and uniformity.

Micro Servo Motor

The motor connects to jumper wires and a 3D-printed plastic slot to function as a gate fulcrum. Blow molding and integrated circuitry are used for production.
